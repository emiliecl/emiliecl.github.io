Urban Selter

Project Description:

This urban shelter is designed to adapt autonomously to its environment, much like a sunflower tracking the sun and banana leaves guiding airflow. Equipped with sensors, the shelter follows the sun’s, optimizing shade throughout the day while also responding to wind dynamics to ensure efficient ventilation.

Project Details:

  • Type: Group project
  • When: First year of Master's
  • Focus: Product design and biomimicry
  • Context: The goal was to design an urban shelter inspired by the nature, creating a dynamic and responsive structure that enhances outdoor comfort.
